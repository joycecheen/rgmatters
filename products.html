Proprietary Hybrid Materials for Radiation Scintillation and Direct Detection

OMHH Materials

Roentgen Matters offers proprietary, cutting-edge organic metal halide hybrid (OMHH) material samples, engineered for scalable, and high-performance solutions across various radiation scintillation and direct detection applications.

Our materials can detect X-rays, gamma rays, neutrons, beta rays, muons, and other high-energy particles, serving critical needs across homeland security, nuclear industry, nuclear medicine, industrial non-destructive testing, medical imaging, and high energy physics sectors etc.

Available Materials Forms

Material samples are available in single crystals, powders, glass, amorphous thin films, flexible sheets, inks, and solutions. Our materials can meet a wide range of detector designs and manufacturing needs.
